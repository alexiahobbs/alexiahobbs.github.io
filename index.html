Nala Hobbs

1994 - 2003

Not only a pet, but also a best friend and sister

Nala was a loving cocker spaniel that served as a companion for Lexi for many years. It was evident their bond was unbreakable but their companionship
was sealed when Nala defended Lexi's honor in her first neighborhood scuffle. It was locked, Nala was Lexi's dog 4 life (in more ways than one!)

  • 2004: Nala is laid to rest
